Our Journey ❤️

Every beautiful love story has a beginning, and ours began in the most unexpected way.

We first knew each other as cousins. At that time, neither of us knew that life was quietly writing a much bigger story for us.

As time passed, we slowly became best friends. We talked for hours, laughed at the smallest things, and shared everything with each other—our childhood memories, our past, our dreams, our fears, and the future we hoped for. There were no secrets between us.

One thing that always made me smile was when you ordered food for me. It wasn't just about the food; it was the care and love behind those little gestures that made me feel so special.

Most of our conversations happened over phone calls. Even though we were miles apart, every call made the distance feel smaller. Those conversations became the best part of my day, and hearing your voice always brought me peace.

Then came the day we had been waiting for—we finally met after such a long time.

Those two days with you became some of the most unforgettable moments of my life. Every smile, every conversation, every laugh, and every second we spent together is a memory I'll cherish forever.

When it was time to leave, I couldn't explain what I was feeling. It felt like I was leaving behind a part of myself. That's when I realized that my heart had already become attached to yours.

Then came our Truth or Dare game.

It started as a simple game, but it became one of the most meaningful conversations we've ever had. We shared everything with complete honesty, opening our hearts without fear. In those moments, we truly understood each other on a deeper level.

From that day onward, something changed.

Our bond grew stronger with every passing day. Our conversations became longer, our care became deeper, and our love became more real.

And from that moment until today, we've never stopped choosing each other.

This journey is my favorite story because every chapter has you in it. ❤️

I don't know what tomorrow holds, but I know one thing for certain...

I want every new chapter of my life to have you beside me.

I love you, and I promise that this journey won't end here.

It's only the beginning of our forever. ❤️
