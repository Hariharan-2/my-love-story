I'm Sorry ❤️

Before this page ends, there's something I want to say...

I'm sorry.

Most of the time, I never meant to hurt you, but I know there were moments when I made you cry. Seeing you upset because of me is something I never wanted.

Sometimes I became too possessive. Maybe it's because I love you so much and, in my heart, you'll always be my person. But I know love should also bring peace, and I'm trying to be better every day.

Thank you for staying with me through my flaws, my mistakes, and my imperfections.

I'm sorry for every tear, every misunderstanding, and every moment I made your heart heavy.

I promise to love you a little better every single day. ❤️

And once again...

I'm truly sorry, Mine. ❤️
