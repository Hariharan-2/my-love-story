Reasons I Love You ❤️

You've asked me so many times...

"Why do you love me?"

Honestly...

I never had a perfect answer.

Not because I don't love you, but because love isn't something I can explain with words.

Still, I'll try... ❤️

❤️ I love your cute anger. Even when you're scolding me, I'm busy looking at your lips instead of listening. 😅

❤️ I love your kindness. You have the purest heart I've ever known.

❤️ I love your calmness. You bring peace into my life.

❤️ I love the way you speak. I could listen to your voice all day.

❤️ I love your honesty and the loyalty you show to the people you care about.

There are so many more reasons...

But I don't think this website has enough space for all of them.

At the end of the day...

I don't love you because of these reasons.

I love you because you're you. ❤️
